Privacy Policy

Mario Calculator — by Saiful Islam (Sumir)

📋 Overview

Mario Calculator is a free utility app that provides 10 smart calculators including General, EMI, Age, BMI, Calorie, VAT, Stock Market Value, Garments Measurement, Size Chart, and Unit Converter.

This privacy policy explains how we handle your information. In short: we collect nothing.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not show advertisements
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not require account registration or login

💾 Local Storage Only

The app stores your calculation history only on your device using browser localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Can be cleared anytime from within the app
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

📤 WhatsApp Sharing

When you tap the WhatsApp share button, the app opens the WhatsApp application on your device with a pre-formatted message. We do not access your WhatsApp account, contacts, or messages in any way.

🌐 Internet Usage

The app loads Google Fonts (Inter, Hind Siliguri, Press Start 2P) when online. No user data is transmitted during this process.

👶 Children's Privacy

Mario Calculator is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

⚖️ Your Rights

  • Since we collect no data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end
  • You can clear your local history anytime within the app
  • You can uninstall the app at any time
